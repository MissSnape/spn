Skills

I work with weapons like...

Firearms — one of the most useful and effective weapons against supernatural beings. It can be upgraded and used to kill a wide variety of creatures. It helps without upgrading, if you just need firepower. For example, against zombies.
Melee weapons — this melee weapon is used when there is no other weapon or to kill certain creatures, for example, to decapitate vampires. They can use machetes, axes and even swords.

Salt — an effective remedy against ghosts, demons and hellhounds. These creatures cannot pass through the line of salt. Salt can load a gun and also deal damage to these creatures.
Silver. — helps very well against werewolves, werewolves and shapeshifters.
Iron — used as a defense against ghosts, demons and other creatures
Other — various devices that also help against supernatural beings. These are syringes, dead man's blood, holy oil, ropes, paint, kerosene

Supernatural weapon — this is a weapon endowed with supernatural properties and allows you to also kill monsters. There are unique weapons, one of a kind, such as the Colt, the Ruby knife, or the First Blade.
EMF detector, infrared scanner, etc. — electronic devices that can detect the presence of ghosts and other supernatural beings.
